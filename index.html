What do you need to bring to NDA exam Centre?

Candidates have to carry their NDA admit card in the exam centre along with a black ball pen, 2 passport size photo ( the same one which has been used during the application) and a photo ID proof like Aadhar card, voter card, school/college ID card etc.




How can I cancel my NDA exam?

Follow the steps below to withdraw NDA 1 Application Form 2024 without hassles.
Step 1: Go to the official UPSC website, i.e. upsconline.nic.in.
Step 2: Click “NDA Application Form 2024” to withdraw the online form.
Step 3: Separate OTPs will be sent to the registered mobile number and email ID.
